Kappil Beach

4th August 2017 adminPlaces

Kappil beach is about 7 kms north of Varkala. It is a beach – backwater destination. Kappil is not just another tourist spot. It is a must visit place, especially if you are spending time at Varkala beach. Kappil is under Thiruvananthapuram district , Kerala state, India. It is located in Edava Panchayat at the cost of Arabian Sea, near Varkala. Currently kappil is under Attingal Constituency.
